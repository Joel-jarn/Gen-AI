 # Executive Problem Statement
Traditional education systems frequently rely on a one-size-fits-all approach, which fails to
accommodate the unique pacing and comprehension levels of individual students. Educators
struggle to provide 24/7 personalized support, leaving students stuck on complex concepts.
While standard search engines provide information, they lack the pedagogical structure required
to teach a concept effectively without simply giving away the answer.
The objective of this project is to build an intelligent AI Tutoring System utilizing Generative AI
and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG). The platform will ingest specific course
curriculums and use LLMs to provide personalized, conversational tutoring, generate dynamic
quizzes, and adapt its teaching style based on the student's real-time performance.
